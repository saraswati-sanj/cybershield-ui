05 — About

Why We Built This

🛡️

CyberShield

Built to make the internet safer — one tweet at a time. Our mission is to give individuals, schools, and platforms a reliable tool to identify cyberbullying before it causes harm.

Python NLP Twitter API Scikit-learn Flask TensorFlow

Cyberbullying affects over 40% of young people online, and most platforms rely on slow, manual reporting to catch it. CyberShield changes that.

By applying natural language processing and machine learning directly to tweet content, we can detect harmful patterns instantly — whether it's a direct insult, a coordinated pile-on, or a subtle threat disguised as a joke.

The tool is designed to be transparent: every verdict comes with a confidence score and indicator breakdown so you understand exactly why a tweet was flagged. No black boxes.

Whether you're a researcher studying online harm, a school counselor monitoring student safety, or a developer building a safer platform — CyberShield is built for you.
